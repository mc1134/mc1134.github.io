Bottles to Models

Overview

We turn plastic bottles into 3D printer filament.

Mission

"To transform plastic waste, such as bottles and 3D printing scrap, into reusable material for 3D printing."

Vision

"To promote more sustainable usage of plastic throughout UCSD's campus,
to implement our system throughout campus and possibly at nearby middle/high schools."
